Frequently asked

Questions we hear most.

If something isn't covered, the simplest path is a short call.

Speak with us
Minimum transaction size? +
We focus on transactions in the upper five-figure range and above. Smaller flows accommodated for existing clients only.
What documentation do you need? +
Individuals: ID, proof of residence, source-of-funds. Corporates: incorporation documents, beneficial owners, financial statements. Exact list provided at onboarding.
Which jurisdictions? +
Most major jurisdictions across North America, Europe, the United Kingdom, the Middle East, and Asia-Pacific. Sanctioned jurisdictions excluded.
How is pricing determined? +
Quoted per transaction or as a tiered schedule based on volume, currencies, and rails. Indicative pricing before any commitment.
How long does onboarding take? +
Individuals: 3–5 business days. Corporates: 1–3 weeks, depending on structure complexity.
Do you provide custody? +
No. Assets held only transiently for execution, with daily reconciliation and segregation from corporate funds.
