1. Introduction

This Cookie Notice explains how Ardenovirex Inc., trading as Ardenpay, uses cookies and similar technologies on the ardenpay.com website. It should be read together with our Privacy Policy.

2. What are cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to enable websites to function, to remember your preferences, and to provide information to website operators about how their sites are used. We may also use other similar technologies, such as pixels and local storage, for similar purposes.

3. Categories of cookies we use

The cookies we use fall into the following categories:

  • Strictly necessary: required for the website to operate. These cannot be disabled in our systems and do not require consent.
  • Analytics: used to understand how visitors interact with the site, in aggregate and anonymised form, to help us improve our content. These are deployed only with your consent where required.

We do not use advertising or third-party marketing cookies on this website.

4. Controlling cookies

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies or to be alerted when cookies are being sent. Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of this website.

5. Changes

We may update this Cookie Notice from time to time. The current version is the one published on this page.

6. Contact

For questions about our use of cookies, please contact privacy@ardenpay.com.
